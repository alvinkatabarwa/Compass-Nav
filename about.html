Compass Navigation Software

The main aim of the software is to streamline the process of new students familiarizing themselves with the campus grounds as well as guests visiting the campus for the first time. The softwares scope includes the providing of maps of the entire campus


Our Main stakeholders are :

Students who need to navigate the campus everyday.


Students would greatly benefit from a campus navigation app by easily finding their way around campus, especially during their first year when everything is new. The app can provide real-time directions to classrooms, libraries, dining halls, and other facilities, ensuring that students can get to their destinations on time. Additionally, it can help students discover new resources and events happening on campus, enhancing their overall college experience by ensuring they donâ€™t miss out on opportunities and services available to them.

Prospective parents and students.


Prospective parents and students visiting the campus can benefit from a navigation app by making their campus tours more organized and informative. The app can guide them through key locations such as academic buildings, dormitories, and recreational facilities, offering detailed descriptions and information about each site. This self-guided tour feature can help them better understand the campus layout and feel more confident about their decision to join the community. Additionally, the app can provide information about nearby accommodations and dining options, making their visit more comfortable and enjoyable.

Staff and Employees of the African Leadership university.


For staff and employees, a campus navigation app can streamline their daily routines and improve efficiency. It can help them locate meeting rooms, administrative offices, and other essential areas quickly, reducing the time spent searching for locations. The app can also integrate with their schedules to provide timely reminders and directions to meetings or events. Furthermore, in case of emergencies, the app can guide them to the nearest exits or safety points, ensuring a safer work environment.
